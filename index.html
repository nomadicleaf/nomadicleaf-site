About Nomadic Leaf

Nomadic Leaf brings travelers and locals together for curated, compliant, luxury-leaning experiences in Las Vegas. Think camera-ready moments, smooth timing, and hosts who keep the vibe high while the logistics stay invisible.

We partner only with licensed venues and trusted operators so each experience feels premium, safe, and intentional. From lounge-based smoke sessions to immersive nights out, everything is designed to feel elevated and effortless.

We are proud supporters of cannabis history, past, present, and future. Education is part of the fun, which is why select experiences feature cannabis trivia and prizes ranging from Nomadic Leaf items to wellness and accessory drops and partner-supported giveaways.

Nomadic Leaf doesn’t sell cannabis. Purchases happen directly at licensed partner venues when permitted by law. On-bus or public consumption is prohibited. 21+ with valid government ID.

Who it’s for: couples, friend groups, birthdays, team outings, visitors who want Vegas with taste, and locals who want a hosted night out without the hassle.

Late/No-Show: Start times matter. Missed segments or no-shows are non-refundable.

Route Changes: Venues, timing, or included elements may change due to availability, traffic, or safety. Equivalent value will be provided where possible.

Purchases: All product purchases are made directly with licensed partners and are subject to their policies and taxes.

VIP Add-On: Limited inventory. Perks listed in each event. No cash equivalent; non-refundable within 72 hours.

Content & Privacy: Photos/video may be captured for brand recaps. Tell a host on arrival if you prefer not to be featured.

Force Majeure: We aren’t liable for delays or changes caused by events outside our control (weather, traffic, venue incidents, etc.).

Peak-Date Surcharge: A $20 per-guest surcharge may apply on select peak dates (e.g., F1, CES).
