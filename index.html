About Nomadic Leaf

We’re a nomad-hearted experience born in Las Vegas—designed to bring people together from everywhere. Travelers and locals meet as one crew for a curated, immersive cannabis-friendly journey: premium dispensary access, compliant lounge moments, and culture-soaked stops you’ll actually want in your camera roll. We handle the details; you make the memories.

What We Do

  • Curated itineraries: behind-the-scenes dispensary access, compliant lounge sessions, glass artistry pop-ups, immersive art (e.g., AREA15).
  • High-touch hosting: friendly on-mic hosts keep timing tight, energy high, and photos rolling.
  • VIP options: priority boarding, premium seating, and a VIP swag experience with partner goodies.
  • Content-ready moments: iconic backdrops, quick challenges, and creators’ nights.

Why Nomadic Leaf

  • Luxury feel, local savvy—boutique routes and a trusted partner network.
  • Community first—connecting visitors and locals through inclusive shared experiences.
  • Compliance & safety—licensed venues, trained staff, clear guest guidance.
  • Value for partners—qualified traffic and repeatable, on-brand moments.

Compliance & Safety

  • Age 21+ with valid government ID.
  • No public or on-bus consumption. Consumption only at licensed venues when permitted.
  • Guests purchase directly from partner dispensaries; Nomadic Leaf does not sell cannabis.
  • Respect venue rules and Nevada law; our team will guide every step.

Who It’s For

  • Vegas visitors and locals seeking a premium, hassle-free night out.
  • Couples, friend groups, birthdays, and team outings.
  • Curious first-timers and seasoned enthusiasts who value style and compliance.

Meet Your Twin Hosts

Nandi & Inky

Nandi and Inky are identical twins with a magnetic energy that instantly lights up any room — and now, any tour bus. Born in Long Beach, CA and raised between San Francisco and Arizona, they bring West Coast creativity and humor to everything they do.

Together, they’ve spent years working as art and print models, often posing as a pair and building a natural chemistry that now translates into on-tour hosting. Known for their quick wit, love of movie quotes, and endless laughter, they’re natural entertainers who keep energy high while making every guest feel welcome.

Both are passionate artists:

  • Nandi is a self-taught cake artist and edible creator, founder of The Cake Trap…
  • Inky is a professional art model with 10+ years of experience…

As a duo, they’re foodies with a sweet tooth, natural comedians, and lovers of art, travel, and unique experiences. Their favorite way to consume? Edibles. Their favorite way to connect? Laughter.

With degrees in fashion design and fine art, plus years of creative hustle, Nandi and Inky are the perfect ambassadors for Nomadic Leaf. They’re not just hosts — they’re guides, performers, and curators of the journey.

Founder

Jerome Butler — Founder

Jerome Butler founded Nomadic Leaf after years of living a truly nomadic life. Raised across multiple states and shaped by service in the U.S. Navy, he traveled widely—by air, sea, and underwater—developing a deep appreciation for culture, craft, and the logistics that make great experiences feel effortless. That perspective now informs a curated luxury experience designed to connect locals and visitors in the same moment, with the same sense of belonging—Where the Leaf Takes You.

Jerome is a long-time advocate for the cannabis industry, believing it’s one of the defining industries of now and the future when it’s approached with respect, hospitality standards, and community. Professionally, he built a broad, cross-functional foundation—business consulting, marketing, sales, partnerships, HR, and SEO—so he understands both the front-of-house warmth guests feel and the back-of-house systems that keep an experience on time and on brand. That combination of passion and discipline is the through line of his work.

Las Vegas is where the moving finally stopped and the vision focused. For Jerome, luxury isn’t loud; it’s thoughtful—curation over clutter, care over flash, and a sense that every guest was expected. He brings that lens to Nomadic Leaf’s host culture: high-energy, welcoming, and intentional about creating camera-worthy moments that still feel human.

When he isn’t refining guest touchpoints or deepening relationships with community partners, Jerome is likely scouting light for the next photo stop, swapping stories with locals, or planning a quick trip to his favorite place, Hawai‘i. His north star is simple: build experiences that turn strangers into crew—and let the journey do the rest.
