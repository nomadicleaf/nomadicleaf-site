Below is a typical flow. Exact timing and stops may change based on partner availability and special events—always check “Tickets & Events” for the date you choose.

Sample Flow

  • Pickup & Welcome — Location provided on confirmation (hosted meet-and-greet, house rules, intro).
  • Fremont Experience — Iconic downtown energy, lights, quick shopping & photo ops (≈30 min).
  • Compliant Cannabis Lounge — Hosted lounge time with one included item per guest; VIPs receive an additional item (≈60 min). Optional visit to a compliant dispensary on-site (≈30 min).
  • In-Transit Fun — Trivia, cannabis knowledge games, and giveaways for exclusive swag.
  • AREA15 — Dinner at The Beast (included), plus an immersive show and additional activities.
  • Return — Drop-off at the original meeting point.

What’s Included

  • Hosted experience with energetic twin hosts.
  • Dinner at The Beast at AREA15 (included with your ticket).
  • Immersive show + additional activities at AREA15.
  • One lounge item included for each guest; VIPs receive an additional item.
  • Games, trivia, and exclusive swag prizes.

Note: Nomadic Leaf does not sell or provide cannabis products. Any consumption is restricted to compliant venues only.

FAQ

Is this a cannabis tour?

It’s a curated Las Vegas experience that includes a visit to a licensed, compliant cannabis lounge. No cannabis is provided or sold by Nomadic Leaf; consumption is only at compliant venues.

What’s the pickup location?

The exact meeting point is shared on your confirmation. Please arrive 10–15 minutes early.

Age & ID

21+ only with valid government-issued photo ID.

Accessibility

If you have accessibility needs, email us before booking so we can confirm accommodations on your date.
