About Nomadic Leaf

Nomadic Leaf brings travelers and locals together for curated, compliant, luxury-leaning experiences in Las Vegas. The vibe is premium, the timing is smooth, and the details are handled so the night feels effortless.

We partner with licensed venues and trusted operators to create experiences that feel intentional from the moment you arrive. Our signature style blends hosted energy, elevated pacing, and camera-ready moments that still feel authentic.

We are proud supporters of cannabis history, past, present, and future. Education is part of the fun, which is why select experiences include trivia, prizes, and partner-supported giveaways that keep the energy high without feeling forced.

  • Curated, luxury-leaning, compliance-first experiences
  • Visitors and locals together, same crew energy
  • Photo-friendly pacing and premium partner venues

Nomadic Leaf does not sell cannabis. Any purchases happen directly at licensed partner venues when permitted by law. Public or on-bus consumption is prohibited. 21+ with valid government ID.

Terms & Policies

Cancellations: Refundable up to 72 hours before start time (unless a ticketing platform states otherwise on its checkout page). Within 72 hours, tickets are non-refundable but are transferable to another 21+ guest.

Compliance: Follow all venue rules and Nevada law. Nomadic Leaf does not sell cannabis. Public or on-bus consumption is prohibited.

ID & Refusal of Service: Valid government ID required. Guests who are visibly intoxicated, disruptive, or non-compliant may be denied entry or removed without refund.

Late/No-Show: Start times matter. Missed segments or no-shows are non-refundable.

Route Changes: Venues, timing, or included elements may change due to availability, traffic, or safety. Equivalent value will be provided where possible.

Purchases: All product purchases are made directly with licensed partners and are subject to their policies and taxes.

VIP Add-On: Limited inventory. Perks listed in each event. No cash equivalent; non-refundable within 72 hours.

Content & Privacy: Photos/video may be captured for brand recaps. Tell a host on arrival if you prefer not to be featured.

Force Majeure: We aren’t liable for delays or changes caused by events outside our control (weather, traffic, venue incidents, etc.).

Peak-Date Surcharge: A $20 per-guest surcharge may apply on select peak dates (e.g., F1, CES).
