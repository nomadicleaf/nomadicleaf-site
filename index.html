About Nomadic Leaf

We’re a nomad-hearted experience born in Las Vegas—designed to bring people together from everywhere. Travelers and locals meet as one crew for a curated, immersive cannabis-friendly journey: compliant lounge moments, premium partner access, and culture-soaked stops you’ll actually want in your camera roll. We handle the details; you make the memories.

What We Do

  • Curated itineraries: compliant lounge sessions, premium stops (e.g., immersive art at AREA15).
  • High-touch hosting: friendly on-mic hosts keep timing tight, energy high, and photos rolling.
  • VIP options: priority boarding, premium seating, and a Nomadic Leaf Swag Bag with partner goodies.
  • Content-ready moments: iconic backdrops, quick challenges, and creators’ nights.

Why Nomadic Leaf

  • Luxury feel, local savvy—boutique routes and a trusted partner network.
  • Community first—connecting visitors and locals through inclusive shared experiences.
  • Compliance & safety—licensed venues, trained staff, clear guest guidance.
  • Value for partners—qualified traffic and repeatable, on-brand moments.

Compliance & Safety

  • Age 21+ with valid government ID.
  • No public or on-bus consumption. Consumption only at licensed venues when permitted.
  • Guests purchase directly from partner dispensaries; Nomadic Leaf does not sell cannabis.
  • Respect venue rules and Nevada law; our team will guide every step.

Who It’s For

  • Vegas visitors and locals seeking a premium, hassle-free night out.
  • Couples, friend groups, birthdays, and team outings.
  • Curious first-timers and seasoned enthusiasts who value style and compliance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is pickup?

Pickup details are shared in your confirmation email 48 hours prior to the tour for timing and crowd-flow reasons.

Is this 21+?

Yes. All guests must be 21+ with a valid government-issued ID.

Can I consume on the bus?

No. There is no public or on-bus consumption. Consumption only at licensed venues when permitted by venue rules.

What’s included?

Compliant lounge time, optional dispensary visit, on-bus games with prizes, and an upscale dinner at The Beast (AREA15), followed by an immersive show & activities. VIP add-on offers priority seating and a Nomadic Leaf Swag Bag.

Refunds & cancellations

Cancellations 72 hours or more before your tour are eligible for a refund (less applicable processing fees). Inside 72 hours, tickets are non-refundable but may be transferable if space allows.

Accessibility & special requests

Email info@nomadicleaf.com and we’ll do our best to accommodate.
